# Global Corporate Expense Policy

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines the standard guidelines and policies for employee expenses globally.

## 2. Food Category
- **Breakfast:** Maximum allowable reimbursement is $20.
- **Lunch:** Maximum allowable reimbursement is $30.
- **Dinner:** The maximum allowable reimbursement for dinner in standard regions is $50. For high-cost metro cities (e.g., London, NYC, San Francisco), the limit is up to $100.
- **Alcohol Restriction:** Alcohol is strictly prohibited from reimbursement under all individual meal claims. No exceptions will be made.
- **Team Building:** Team building expenses or client dinners must include at least two or more participants. Any single-person meal claimed as "Team Building" is strictly prohibited and must be flagged as fraudulent or erroneous reporting.

## 3. Lodging
- Hotel stays require pre-approval from management.
- The standard base nightly limit is $250.
- Stays exceeding $400 require vice-president signature and prior written authorization.

## 4. Transport and Travel
- Rideshares (Uber/Lyft/Ola) are permitted for direct transit to client meetings or airports.
- Airport transfers must utilize standard economy or sedans (no luxury/premier vehicles).
- Digital or physical receipts are absolutely required for any transit expense exceeding $10.
